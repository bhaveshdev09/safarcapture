Kasol Kheerganga Trek

Himchal Pradesh, India

(1,186 Reviews)

Highlight

Kasol, a picturesque hamlet nestled in the Kullu district of Himachal Pradesh, is a traveler's paradise. This serene destination is situated in the enchanting Parvati Valley, cradled by the meandering Parvati River. If you're seeking an adventure that immerses you in the breathtaking landscapes of Himachal Pradesh, the Kasol Kheerganga Trek is your ticket to an unforgettable experience.

Iternary

Day 0: DEPART FROM DELHI BY 06:00 PM

• We depart from Delhi around 6 PM in an AC Vehicle. (Know the true value of time snatch, seize, and enjoy every moment of it.)
• Pit stop for dinner at any decent roadside restaurant.

Day 1: REACH KASOL | KASOL EXPLORATION | OVERNIGHT STAY AT KASOL

• Check in to the hotel, freshen up & have breakfast or lunch (Depending on the Arrival).
• After having breakfast, go for a hike to Chalal Village and capture some beautiful views of Parvati Valley.br
• In the evening, we will visit the Local market, try different cuisines at the local Hippie Cafe's.
• Return to the Hotel & Overnight stay in Kasol.

Day 2: TREK TO KHEERGANGA | OVERNIGHT CAMP STAY AT KHEERGANGA

• After early morning breakfast, a briefing session by the trek leader sharing all the necessary details about the Kheerganga trek would be provided.
• Trek will start from a village called Barsheni (22 km from Kasol) and you will be transferred to Barsheni in private vehicles.
• It takes around 5-7 hours to reach the spring.
• Check-in to the camps and head to the springs for an exquisite swim.
• Spend the night under a sky full of stars and surrounded by mountains.
• Overnight stay in Kheerganga.

Day 3: TREK DOWN TO BARSHENI | MANIKARAN GURUDWARA | TIME TO HEAD BACK

• Wake up to see the first ray of sun falling on the mighty Himalayas.
• Post breakfast, trek back to Barshaini (the same trail).
• Drive to Kasol from Barshaini.
• On the way, visit the holy Manikaran Gurudwara, famous for it's natural hotsprings.
• In the Evening, We'll board an overnight bus for our return journey to Delhi.

Day 4: REACH BACK TO DELHI BY MORNING

• Reach Delhi in the morning
